Who We Are

For more than thirty years, we have been one of the leading metal works for the most important government and private projects in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We are the "metal design factory" specializing in the metal works industry, especially in the design, manufacture and implementation of CNC laser cutting doors, gates and metal facades with a team of the best designers, engineering and technical personnel.

Under the slogan "Design Innovation and Manufacturing Quality", our factory has had a major goal since its inception, which is to form and build a trustworthy reputation in the metallurgical industry, to become a significant contributor to the Kingdom's most important projects and businesses. We look forward to meeting all the requirements of our customers locally and globally by manufacturing metal works with global standards.
